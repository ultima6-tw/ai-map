# ⚙️ Harness Engineering

The third and most advanced layer, focusing on **"building automated feedback loops."**

## 📍 Core Concepts
- **Core Concept**: Shifting from human review to automated frameworks (Harness). After AI generates code or analysis, the harness automatically runs tests, captures errors, and feeds them back to the AI for autonomous repair.
- **Goal**: Achieve true "Self-Driving" development capable of managing massive, million-line scale projects and complex workflows.
