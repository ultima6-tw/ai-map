# AI Applications & Scientific Breakthroughs

A curated list of AI impact in scientific research, material science, and corporate efficiency.

- **[Researchers Create First AI for Generative Polymer Design](https://coe.gatech.edu/news/2026/03/researchers-create-first-ai-generative-polymer-design)**
    - Georgia Tech researchers developed the first AI for "Generative Polymer Design," enabling the automatic creation of materials with specific properties.
- **[Accelerating scientific breakthroughs with an AI co-scientist](https://research.google/blog/accelerating-scientific-breakthroughs-with-an-ai-co-scientist/)**
    - Google Research explores the role of AI as a "co-scientist," helping humans handle data, form hypotheses, and accelerate workflows.
- **[How generative AI can help scientists synthesize complex materials | MIT News](https://news.mit.edu/2026/how-generative-ai-can-help-scientists-synthesize-complex-materials-0202)**
    - MIT describes how generative AI assists in synthesizing complex materials, reducing experimental cycles from years to months.
- **[A new generative AI approach to predicting chemical reactions | MIT News](https://news.mit.edu/2025/generative-ai-approach-to-predicting-chemical-reactions-0903)**
    - Another MIT breakthrough: Using generative AI to predict the outcomes of chemical reactions, enhancing precision in experimental design.
- **[AskDona（アスクドナ）- 法人向け生成AIプラットフォーム](https://askdona.com/)**
    - A Japanese generative AI platform for corporations, focusing on secure data management and office automation.
- **[Activities | Department of Robotic Science - Tokyo Tech](https://rosci.org/en/activities/)**
