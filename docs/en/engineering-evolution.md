# 🤖 The 3 Layers of AI Engineering Evolution

From simple chat interactions to autonomous maintenance of million-line codebases, AI engineering is undergoing a paradigm shift from prompt design to automated frameworks.

## 📍 Three Core Layers

### 1. Prompt Engineering
The first layer of AI interaction, focusing on **"how to ask."**
- **Core Techniques**: Structured instructions, Chain-of-Thought (CoT), Few-shot learning, and Fine-tuning strategies.
- **Advanced Resources**:
    - [9 Hour AI Prompt Engineering Course In 20 Minutes](https://youtu.be/p09yRj47kNM?si=mKoaLJe5cugXFRxD)
    - [The Fatal Flaw of LoRA? Breaking Catastrophic Forgetting with "Share"](https://youtu.be/x5nEkcLsrCc?si=skbFmd7Cn_-rwAmc): Exploring methods to enhance model expertise via fine-tuning.

### 2. Context Engineering
The second layer of AI collaboration, focusing on **"providing the right background."**
- **Core Technologies**: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), Vector Databases, Long-term Memory Management, Context Window Optimization, and Knowledge Graph (KG) integration.
- **Advanced Resources**:
    - [【Ultimate Guide to Knowledge Graphs】From Scratch to LLM Integration](knowledge-graph.md): A comprehensive guide to building intelligent knowledge bases.
    - [GPT-5 Lost? Princeton's 14B Small Model Hack: Knowledge Graph + RLVR](https://youtu.be/8e9E6TqeZPA?si=d1F8b9JiBqk4iMMk): Techniques to improve factual accuracy through Knowledge Graphs.
- **Practice**: OpenClaw's Knowledge Graph (KG) integration, [QMD local document search](qmd.md).

### 3. Harness Engineering
The third and most advanced layer, focusing on **"building automated feedback loops."**
- **Core Concept**: Shifting from human review to automated frameworks (Harness). After AI generates code, the harness automatically runs tests, captures errors, and feeds them back to the AI for autonomous repair.
- **Goal**: Achieve true "Self-Driving" development capable of managing massive, million-line scale projects.
- **Case Study**: [The 3 Layers of AI Engineering: From Prompt to Harness](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=urwDLyNa9FU)
