# 🎨 AI Image Generation

AI image generation has entered an era of "photorealistic" results and high "prompt adherence."

## Flagship Tools
- **[Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com/)**: The king of artistic aesthetics, excelling in composition and color grading.
- **[Flux.1](https://blackforestlabs.ai/)**: A 2024 breakthrough open-source model that fixes common AI issues like rendering text and realistic hands.
- **[DALL-E 3](https://openai.com/dall-e-3)**: Integrated into ChatGPT, featuring the strongest semantic understanding without needing complex prompts.

## Professional Techniques
- **Control & Detail**: Use [Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai/) with **ControlNet** for precise control over poses, architecture, and outlines.
- **Video Generation**: Pair with [Luma Dream Machine](https://lumalabs.ai/dream-machine) or [Runway Gen-3 Alpha](https://runwayml.com/gen-3-alpha) to turn static images into cinematic motion.
