# 🕸️ Knowledge Graph

## 🧠 Why OpenClaw Needs Knowledge Graphs

In OpenClaw's architecture, basic Vector Search is insufficient for handling complex multi-node states or long-term behavioral dependencies. By integrating a Knowledge Graph:
1. **Fact Accuracy**: Ensures knowledge consistency across sessions.
2. **Logical Reasoning**: Enables the AI to perform Multi-hop Reasoning along the relationships within the graph.
