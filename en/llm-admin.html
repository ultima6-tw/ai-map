🗂️ Administrative Tasks

Official documents, correspondence, meeting minutes, reports — make everyday admin easier and more efficient.

Official Document Drafting

Describe the purpose and key content of the document, let AI generate a properly formatted draft, then review and finalize.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please help me draft a formal official document. Issuing unit: Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica Recipient: [Fill in] Subject: [One sentence describing the purpose, e.g.: Invitation to the 2026 Annual Symposium] Key content: [Bullet points, e.g.: date, venue, registration method, deadline] Please use standard formal document format, maintain a formal tone, and keep clear logical structure between paragraphs.
⚠️ After drafting, please have a supervisor review it to confirm the content and format meet institutional requirements before sending.

Meeting Minutes Organization

Give AI your rough meeting notes or transcript and have it organize them into clear, structured meeting minutes.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please organize the following meeting notes into formal meeting minutes. Format requirements: - Meeting name, date, attendees (preserve the information I provide) - Discussion items: organized by topic, summarizing key discussion points for each - Resolutions: bullet points, noting responsible persons and deadlines (if applicable) - Action items: bullet points Notes content: [Paste notes or transcript here]
⚠️ After organizing, verify that the resolutions match the actual discussion — avoid transcription errors.

Correspondence Drafting

Describe the purpose and tone of the letter, and let AI generate an appropriate draft in Chinese or English.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please help me write an email. Recipient: [e.g.: International collaboration partner] Purpose: [e.g.: Confirm details of an upcoming visit, including schedule, venue, and reception arrangements] Tone: Formal but friendly Our institution: Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica Please begin with "Dear [Name]," and close with "Best regards."

Event Announcement

Provide basic event information and let AI generate a ready-to-use announcement suitable for bulletin boards or email distribution.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please write an event announcement in a formal but welcoming tone, suitable for sending to all institute members. Event details: - Event name: [Fill in] - Date and time: [Fill in] - Venue: [Fill in] - Audience: All IAMS members - Registration: [Fill in, or state no registration required] - Contact: [Fill in] - Other notes: [Fill in or leave blank] Please include a title, body, and closing, with contact information at the end.

Academic Conference Registration Website

Provide event details and let AI generate a complete static registration page (HTML) that can be hosted on GitHub Pages or an institutional website — no coding required.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please create a conference registration webpage (single HTML file with embedded CSS). Conference details: - Conference name: [e.g.: 2026 IAMS Annual Symposium] - Date: [Fill in] - Venue: [Fill in] - Organizer: Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica - Registration deadline: [Fill in] Registration form fields: - Name (required) - Department / Lab affiliation (required) - Position (Researcher / Postdoc / Graduate student / Administrative staff / Other) - Email (required) - Vegetarian meal required (Yes / No) - Notes (optional) Design requirements: - Clean and professional, white and navy color scheme - Mobile-responsive - After form submission, display "Thank you for registering — we will confirm by email" (no backend needed; simulate with JavaScript)
⚠️ This is a static form; data will not be automatically collected. For real registrations, pair with Google Forms or Formspree.

Report Narrative Summary

Give AI numerical data or a table and have it generate a clear written explanation suitable for reports or presentations.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Below is a set of statistical data. Please write a 100–150 word narrative summary suitable for an annual report. Requirements: - Objectively present the main trends in the data - Highlight the most noteworthy changes - Formal tone; do not draw inferences beyond the data Data: [Paste table or numbers here]
⚠️ Verify that the AI's narrative is consistent with the raw data — do not allow it to infer or supplement figures.

Usage Reminders

✅

Drafts Only

All AI-generated documents and letters are drafts. Before sending, always have the responsible person review the content, format, and accountability.

🔒

No Personal Data

When drafting letters or documents, avoid entering others' personal information (e.g., ID numbers, contact details) into online AI services.

📋

Institutional Rules Take Priority

Document format and language should follow your institution's current regulations. AI output is for reference only — adjust according to actual requirements.
