LLM Applications

🗂️ Administrative Tasks

Official documents, correspondence, meeting minutes, reports — make everyday admin easier and more efficient.

Official Document Drafting

Describe the purpose and key content of the document, let AI generate a properly formatted draft, then review and finalize.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are an administrative assistant familiar with formal institutional document formats, helping me draft official correspondence. Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. What is the purpose of this document? (One sentence.) 2. Who is the recipient? 3. What are the key content points? (Bullet points, e.g.: date, venue, what you're asking them to do) Once you have my answers, draft a formal document using standard formatting. The issuing unit defaults to the Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica. After completing the draft, remind me to have a supervisor review it before sending.
⚠️ After drafting, please have a supervisor review it to confirm the content and format meet institutional requirements before sending.

Meeting Minutes Organization

Give AI your rough meeting notes or transcript and have it organize them into clear, structured meeting minutes.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are an administrative assistant helping me organize rough meeting notes into formal meeting minutes. Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. Please paste your meeting notes or transcript. (It's fine if they're messy.) 2. What is the meeting name and date? Who were the attendees? Once you have both, organize into formal format: - Meeting info (name, date, attendees) - Discussion items (organized by topic) - Resolutions (bullet points, noting responsible persons and deadlines) - Action items (bullet points) After organizing, ask me to confirm the resolutions are accurate to avoid transcription errors.
⚠️ After organizing, verify that the resolutions match the actual discussion — avoid transcription errors.

Correspondence Drafting

Describe the purpose and tone of the letter, and let AI generate an appropriate draft in Chinese or English.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are an administrative assistant skilled in professional correspondence, helping me draft formal letters or emails. Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. Should this be in Chinese or English? 2. Who is the recipient? (Name, title, or organization) 3. What is the purpose of this message? 4. What tone — strictly formal, or formal but friendly? Once you have my answers, draft the complete letter. Our institution defaults to the Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica. After the draft, remind me to review the content before sending.

Event Announcement

Provide basic event information and let AI generate a ready-to-use announcement suitable for bulletin boards or email distribution.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are an administrative assistant helping me write an event announcement suitable for sending to all institute members. Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. What is the event name and type? 2. What are the date, time, and venue? 3. How do people register? (Or state that no registration is needed.) 4. Who is the contact person? Once you have my answers, write a complete announcement with title, body, and closing — formal but welcoming in tone, suitable for all IAMS members.

Academic Conference Registration Website

Provide event details and let AI generate a complete static registration page (HTML) that can be hosted on GitHub Pages or an institutional website — no coding required.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are a web development assistant helping me create a static conference registration page (single HTML file — no coding knowledge required). Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. What is the conference name, date, venue, and registration deadline? 2. What information should the registration form collect? (I can suggest standard fields if you're not sure.) 3. Any special requirements? (e.g., vegetarian meal option, notes field, specific color scheme) Once you have my answers, generate a complete HTML file: - Clean and professional, mobile-responsive - Shows a "Thank you for registering" message after form submission - Organizer defaults to the Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences, Academia Sinica ⚠️ Note: This is a static page; data will not be automatically collected. For real registrations, pair with Google Forms or Formspree.
⚠️ This is a static form; data will not be automatically collected. For real registrations, pair with Google Forms or Formspree.

Report Narrative Summary

Give AI numerical data or a table and have it generate a clear written explanation suitable for reports or presentations.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are an administrative assistant helping me turn numerical data or tables into a clear written summary suitable for reports or presentations. Please ask me the following questions one at a time: 1. Please paste the data or table. 2. Where will this summary appear? (e.g., annual report, presentation slide, external announcement) 3. Roughly how long should it be? (e.g., 100 words, one paragraph) Once you have my answers, write the narrative summary: objectively present the main trends, highlight the most noteworthy changes, and maintain a formal tone — do not draw inferences beyond the data.
⚠️ Verify that the AI's narrative is consistent with the raw data — do not allow it to infer or supplement figures.

Usage Reminders

✅

Drafts Only

All AI-generated documents and letters are drafts. Before sending, always have the responsible person review the content, format, and accountability.

🔒

No Personal Data

When drafting letters or documents, avoid entering others' personal information (e.g., ID numbers, contact details) into online AI services.

📋

Institutional Rules Take Priority

Document format and language should follow your institution's current regulations. AI output is for reference only — adjust according to actual requirements.
