📄 Writing & Text Processing

Save enormous amounts of time on reading, translation, and writing.

Manuscript Editing

Hand your draft to AI for editing, specify the journal style, preserve your data and terminology, and only adjust grammar and fluency.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
You are a senior editor specializing in physics and chemistry journals. Please edit the following paragraph to match the academic style of Physical Review Letters. Requirements: - Preserve all data, formulas, and technical terms — do not alter them - Only adjust grammar, word choice, and sentence flow - Do not add or remove any arguments [Paste paragraph here]
⚠️ After editing, verify technical terms yourself — AI occasionally mistranslates or changes specialized terminology.

Rapid Literature Summary

Give AI a paper abstract (or full text) to extract key information and quickly judge whether the paper is worth reading in full.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please read the following abstract and extract: 1. Research question (one sentence) 2. Methods used 3. Key findings 4. Study limitations Present as bullet points, each no longer than 40 words. [Paste abstract here]
⚠️ If citing, always return to the original text — AI occasionally "supplements" information not present in the source.

Academic Translation

Chinese-to-English or English-to-Chinese translation — specifying the field improves accuracy; always proofread terminology yourself.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please translate the following paragraph into English. This is academic content in the field of physics (condensed matter physics). Use correct technical terminology and match the writing conventions of Nature Physics. [Paste Chinese paragraph here]

Response to Reviewers Draft

Give AI the reviewer comments and your intended response directions, and have it generate a formal Response to Reviewers draft.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
Please help me write a formal response to the following reviewer comment. Reviewer's comment: [Paste reviewer comment here] My intended response: [Describe how you plan to respond, e.g.: We have added experimental details in Chapter 3 of the revised manuscript] Please write in the formal, courteous tone of an academic journal response letter, beginning with "We thank the reviewer for..."
⚠️ The draft must still be revised to reflect your actual revisions — do not submit directly.
