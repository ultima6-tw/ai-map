🧠 Knowledge & Reasoning

Code, brainstorming, concept explanation — your 24-hour research partner.

Code Debugging

Paste the error message and code together, and ask AI to analyze the cause and provide a corrected version.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
The following Python code is producing an error. Please help me debug it. Code: [Paste code here] Error message: [Paste error message here] Please explain step by step: 1. The cause of the error 2. How to fix it 3. The complete corrected code

Experimental Design Brainstorming

Describe your research problem and ask AI to propose possible experimental directions or methods, then evaluate feasibility yourself.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
I am researching [research topic], and my current challenge is [describe problem]. Please propose 3–5 possible experimental directions or methods, each including: - Core concept - Potential advantages - Potential challenges Please answer from the perspective of experimental design in physics/chemistry, without considering instrument or budget constraints.
⚠️ AI suggestions need to be evaluated by you for feasibility — it knows nothing about your lab conditions.

Explaining Cross-Disciplinary Concepts

When venturing into unfamiliar fields, ask AI to explain new concepts using analogies from your existing background knowledge.

PROMPT EXAMPLE ▼
I am a researcher with a physics background, reading a biophysics paper. Please use physics language and analogies to explain the concept of "protein folding free energy landscape" to me. Assume I am familiar with statistical mechanics and phase transitions, but not with biological molecular systems.
